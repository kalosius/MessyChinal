Message from the Academic Office - 2025

Dear Students, Parents, and Esteemed Visitors, Welcome to Mityana Secondary School's official website! As we embark on the academic year 2025, we are excited to continue providing quality education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of our students. Our commitment to academic excellence remains at the core of everything we do. This year, we strive to build on our strong foundation by embracing innovation, critical thinking, and holistic development in every student. We encourage a collaborative effort from our teachers, students, and parents to make this journey even more successful and impactful. On this platform, you will find up-to-date information about academic programs, resources, school events, and other important news. We believe in transparency and open communication, and this website is a great tool to keep you informed and engaged with our school community. As we work towards a bright future, we remain focused on ensuring that our students not only excel academically but also grow into responsible, well-rounded individuals who can contribute meaningfully to society. Thank you for being part of the Mityana Secondary School family. We look forward to a year full of growth, achievements, and opportunities.

Warm regards,

Mr. Polisalio
